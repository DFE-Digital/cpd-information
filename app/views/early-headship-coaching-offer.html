Early headship coaching offer

This one-year support package has been tailored to the needs of new headteachers.

It provides structured, unassessed face-to-face support based on evidence about what makes an effective headteacher.

The support you receive will differ depending on your chosen provider.

It may include:

  • group coaching
  • one-to-one support
  • peer networking
To be able to take the early headship coaching offer, you need to be taking or have taken the headship NPQ.

Funding eligibility

To be eligible for funding for the early headship coaching offer, you need to be in your first 5 years of headship at a state-funded school or state-funded organisation that offers 16 to 19 places in England.

Providers

The following providers run the early headship coaching offer:

  • Ambition Institute
  • Best Practice Network (home of Outstanding Leaders Partnership)
  • Church of England
  • Education Development Trust
  • LLSE
  • National Institute of Teaching
  • Teacher Development Trust
  • Teach First
  • UCL Institute of Education

Apply

To apply, you need to:

  • choose a provider - your workplace may be able to help
  • register with the Department for Education
  • apply seperately with your training provider - they’ll send you an application form once you’ve registered
