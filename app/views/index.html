Professional development for teachers and leaders

Choose a national professional qualification (NPQ) to help you:

  • improve your skills
  • progress in your career
  • increase your confidence in your role

NPQs are currently fully funded by the Department for Education, so they’re free to take for most teachers and leaders.

Specialist NPQs

Designed for classroom teachers and leaders.

Leading teaching

Lead the teaching and learning of a subject, year group or phase.

Leading behaviour and culture

Create a culture of good behaviour and high expectations where staff and pupils can succeed.

Leading literacy

Teach and promote literacy across a school, year group, key stage or phase.

Leading teacher development

Become a teacher educator and support teachers in your school to expand their skills.

Leadership NPQs

Designed for teachers and leaders in leadership roles or wanting to achieve leadership skills.

Senior leadership

Develop leadership expertise to improve outcomes for teachers and pupils in your school.

Headship

Learn how to become an expert school leader and headteacher.

Executive leadership

Develop the expertise needed to run a multi-school organisation and lead change effectively.

Early years leadership

Lead high-quality early years education and care and manage staff and organisations effectively.

Support for new headteachers

If you’ve been a headteacher for less than 5 years, you may be eligible for support through the early headship coaching offer.

If you coordinate professional development

Download or print an NPQ prospectus to share with colleagues in your organisation.
